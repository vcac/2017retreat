As we are living busy on this world, sometimes, we hardly make time to think about what God has done to us, how we can live in Him better. In Victoria Chinese Alliance Church, church retreats create a bonding and a community with those people that you are closest to you spiritually, as well as supporting those people and the church itself. During this three-day retreat, you can retreat from your busy life to be surrounding by God's words again.

This Year

  • - back to top

April 28-30, 2017, 34th church retreat will be held at Camp Qwanoes - a beautiful place where we are familiar with. This year theme is Keep Step With The Spirit from Galatians 5:25. You will enjoy four talks by our special speakers, two workshops, one worship night, and relaxed time with our dear God and family. We also have several outdoor activities, so you can spend a great afternoon with your friends in the camp. Join us as a big big family for this wonderful time! Detailed timetable will be posted soon.

Children Programs

  • - back to top

Just like usual, we are preparing some wonderful children programs for your kids. Most of our fun time is consist with Bible study, crafts making, and games. So don't doubt, you children will certainly more enjoy church retreat! If you'd like to help our children ministry, please contact retreat committee! Timetable for children programs will be posted soon.
